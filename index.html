Monetization Restrictions

You cannot monetize directly off .z domains. This includes:

  • Selling the domain itself.
  • Charging for registration or transfer.
  • Running advertisements or paid services, such as:
    • Paywalls.
    • Affiliate marketing.
    • Ads or sponsored content.

Content Monetization

You can still earn through:

  • Donations (e.g., Patreon).
  • Crowdfunding for projects.
  • Selling digital goods or offering services.

For Companies

Commercial use or monopolizing .z domains is strictly prohibited.

Enforcement & Violations

Violations may result in warnings, suspension, or bans.
