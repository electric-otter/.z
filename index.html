Monetization Restrictions

You cannot monetize directly off .z domains. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Selling the domain itself.
  • Charging for domain registration or transferring ownership for profit.
  • Running advertisements or paid services on your domain, such as:
    • Paywalls.
    • Affiliate marketing.
    • Ads or sponsored content.

Content Monetization

While the domain itself cannot be monetized, you can still earn from content hosted on your .z domain through:

  • Donations (e.g., Patreon or similar platforms).
  • Crowdfunding for your projects or services.
  • Selling digital goods, offering consulting, or providing paid services, provided the domain itself remains non-commercial.

What We Don’t Do

The ZPDS service is non-commercial:

  • We will not charge you for using a .z domain.
  • You cannot charge or monetize your domain or attempt to commercialize it for direct or indirect financial gain.

For Companies

Monopolizing .z domains is strictly prohibited. If a company attempts to acquire multiple .z domains or uses them commercially (e.g., selling, leasing, or profiting directly), they will be permanently banned from the ZPDS service.

Enforcement and Violations

Violations may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent bans. We reserve the right to suspend or revoke access to .z domains that violate this policy.

MyDomains

Failure to set the CNAME correctly will result in the service not working.
