CNAME Setup Instructions

To make your .z domain accessible globally, add the following CNAME record:

CNAME yourdomain.z 1.1.1.1

Once you have configured your domain's DNS, your website should be accessible worldwide.

For additional security, we recommend using third-party services or MyDomain Security (currently under development) to protect your site.

Note: MyDomain Security is still in development and unavailable at this time.
